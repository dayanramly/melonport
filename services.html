Cloud & Mobile Development

So the field operators can enter their inspections on the mobile app and access all previous data entered and then seamlessly move to the cloud solution to generate reports.

Internet of Think & Big Data

IoT is the internet of things, think “cloud connected everything”. In the past we have connected sewer inspection cameras to the cloud.

We can remotely debug them, update the software, see where there are and so on. This means our clients don’t have to go out into the field, our customer’s customers get software support and issues resolved right where they are working within minutes instead of hours or days if someone has to fly out. We also cloud-connected a light art installation. Very cool. We will share the details once it is online (next weekend).

In the future we have projects planned to cloud-connect washing machines. So there is a customer who has rooms in student living facilities with shared washing machines. The students will be able to reserve a washing machine online and pay using their credit card instead of by buying coins which are much more annoying.
